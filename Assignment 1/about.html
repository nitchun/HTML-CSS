About Us

Welcome to Nithya's Jewellery and Accessories, your source for all things fashion, fun and personality. I am dedicated to giving you the very best of affordable jewellery and accessories, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded by Nithya Chunduri in a small home office in San Jose, CA, Nithya's Jewellery and Accessories is in the process of making it's way up to the top of the list in it's customers' hearts.

When I first started out, my passion for the things I like while being eco-friendly, providing the best quality jewellery for my customers drove me to do intense research and turn our inspiration into to a good and trusted online store. I now serve customers all over the United States and thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly, fair trade wing of the jewellery industry. I hope you enjoy my products as much as I enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don’t hesitate to contact.
