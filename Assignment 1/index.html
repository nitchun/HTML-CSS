Each piece of my collection is unique and made by hand with love and care. The company is based in the United States, giving access to top grade materials. I also take up orders on jewellery using specific materials like real pearls and gold. All the jewellery is easy and very pleasant to wear!



My collection caters to a variety of occasions. My personal favourites in the jewellery category are the everyday wear and fun and eclectic jewellery.


Everyday wear is suitable when going out just to run errands or to wear to the work place.
Fun and eclectic is for the fashionably brave people. It gives you a chance to stand out in the crowd and make a statement about yourself through jewellery. These pieces are a definite conversation starters. These colourful, vibrant and bold piece are suitable for more relaxed situations like parties or your day out with friends and family.These pieces can certainly liven up your entire wardrobe.

The inspiration of every collection is found in the wonderful diversity of cultures, art, nature, upcoming fashion trends and colours.
