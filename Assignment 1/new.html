Latest Trends

Fringe is in....

One of the biggest trends that we’ve seen is the trend for Fringe jewelry!



Whether it’s strung beads, chain or cord, fringe has been popping up on necklaces, earrings, bracelets and all sorts of accessories.For 2015, we’re expecting to see the trend go bigger and bolder! In the spring you can expect lighter materials such as cord, suede and thread in bight and bold colors complemented with rhinestone cup chain, various kinds of chain and mixed beads.For summer, get ready for even brighter colors, touches of neon and massive amounts of lightweight fringe on collars, wrists and hanging from earrings, as simple strands, tassels, and fringed hoops.

In the fall, there will be a return to metallics and heavier materials with more sparkle combined with suede elements. Combine silver with gold and sparkly crystal for a modern look that’s sure to turn heads. Or create a toned down natural style with metal and cord. For next winter, bundle up with fringe over your sweater and coat as well as in your hair!
